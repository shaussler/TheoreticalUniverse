Translations#

A generic translation in spacetime is expressed with a 4-vector:

\[\begin{split}T^вЩѓ = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{1} A^t & вИВ_t \\ A^x & вИВ_x \\ A^y & вИВ_y \\ A^z & вИВ_z \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]

Flattened, the translation expressed in differential form is:

\[\begin{split}T^вЩ≠ = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{2} & A^t & dt \\ - & A^x & dx \\ - & A^y & dy \\ - & A^z & dz \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]
