\[\begin{split}F^{ÔÖ»ÔÖ»} = \left[ \begin{aligned} \E^x \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_x - Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_t) \\ \E^y \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_y - Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_t) \\ \E^z \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_z - Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_t) \\ B^x \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_z - Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_y) \\ B^y \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_x - Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_z) \\ B^z \; \frac{1}{2} & (Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_y - Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_x) \\ \end{aligned} \right]\end{split}\]

Reorder

\[\begin{split}F^{ÔÖ»ÔÖ»} = \frac{1}{2} \left[ \begin{aligned} \E^x \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_x & - \E^x \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_t \\ \E^y \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_y & - \E^y \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_t \\ \E^z \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_z & - \E^z \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_t \\ B^x \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_z & - B^x \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_y \\ B^y \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_x & - B^y \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_z \\ B^z \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_y & - B^z \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_x \\ \end{aligned} \right]\end{split}\]

Reorder and conclude

\[\begin{split}F^{ÔÖ»ÔÖ»} = \frac{1}{2} \begin{bmatrix} & - \E^x \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_t & - \E^y \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_t & - \E^z \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_t \\ \E^x \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_x & & - B^z \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_x & + B^y \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_x \\ \E^y \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_y & + B^z \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_y & & - B^x \; Ôłé_z Ôłž Ôłé_y \\ \E^z \; Ôłé_t Ôłž Ôłé_z & - B^y \; Ôłé_x Ôłž Ôłé_z & + B^x \; Ôłé_y Ôłž Ôłé_z & \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

With implicit bivector basis, we have the standard representation with abstract index notation, which also permits to verify the calculations here:

\[\begin{split}F_{╬╝╬Ż} = \begin{bmatrix} & - \E^x & - \E^y & - \E^z \\ + \E^x & & - B^z & + B^y \\ + \E^y & + B^z & & - B^x \\ + \E^z & - B^y & + B^x & \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Where the electromagnetic field is related to the doubly contravariant Faraday tensor through:

\[F^{ÔÖ»ÔÖ»} = \frac{1}{2} \: F^{╬╝╬Ż} \: Ôłé_╬╝ Ôłž Ôłé_╬Ż\]

The Hodge dual \(G^{ÔÖşÔÖş}\) of the Faraday 2-form \(F^{ÔÖşÔÖş}\) is:

\[G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = Ôőć F^{ÔÖşÔÖş}\]

Expanded, we obtain:

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{1} B^x \; & dt Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; & dt Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; & dt Ôłž dz \\ \E^x \; & dy Ôłž dz \\ \E^y \; & dz Ôłž dx \\ \E^z \; & dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]

Calculations

Start from the Faraday 2-form

\[\begin{split}F^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{aligned} - & \E^x \; dt Ôłž dx \\ - & \E^y \; dt Ôłž dy \\ - & \E^z \; dt Ôłž dz \\ & B^x \; dy Ôłž dz \\ & B^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ & B^z \; dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{aligned} \right]\end{split}\]

Take the Hodge dual

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = Ôőć F^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = Ôőć \left[ \begin{aligned} - & \E^x \; dt Ôłž dx \\ - & \E^y \; dt Ôłž dy \\ - & \E^z \; dt Ôłž dz \\ & B^x \; dy Ôłž dz \\ & B^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ & B^z \; dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{aligned} \right]\end{split}\]

Distribute the Hodge dual operator

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{aligned} - & \E^x \; Ôőć dt Ôłž dx \\ - & \E^y \; Ôőć dt Ôłž dy \\ - & \E^z \; Ôőć dt Ôłž dz \\ & B^x \; Ôőć dy Ôłž dz \\ & B^y \; Ôőć dz Ôłž dx \\ & B^z \; Ôőć dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{aligned} \right]\end{split}\]

Apply the Hodge dual operator

You can find the Hodge dual of each bivector basis in Minkowski space here.

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{2} - & \E^x \; (-1) & dy Ôłž dz \\ - & \E^y \; (-1) & dz Ôłž dx \\ - & \E^z \; (-1) & dx Ôłž dy \\ & B^x \; (+1) & dt Ôłž dx \\ & B^y \; (+1) & dt Ôłž dy \\ & B^z \; (+1) & dt Ôłž dz \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]

Simplify

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{1} \E^x \; & dy Ôłž dz \\ \E^y \; & dz Ôłž dx \\ \E^z \; & dx Ôłž dy \\ B^x \; & dt Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; & dt Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; & dt Ôłž dz \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]

Reorder

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \left[ \begin{alignedat}{1} B^x \; & dt Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; & dt Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; & dt Ôłž dz \\ \E^x \; & dy Ôłž dz \\ \E^y \; & dz Ôłž dx \\ \E^z \; & dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{alignedat} \right]\end{split}\]

From the dual Faraday 2-form and noting that 2-forms are tensors, we can derive the row/column representation of the doubly covariant dual Faraday tensor.

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \begin{bmatrix} & - B^x \; dx Ôłž dt & - B^y \; dy Ôłž dt & - B^z \; dz Ôłž dt \\ + B^x \; dt Ôłž dx & & - \E^z \; dy Ôłž dx & + \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ + B^y \; dt Ôłž dy & + \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy & & - \E^x \; dz Ôłž dy \\ + B^z \; dt Ôłž dz & - \E^y \; dx Ôłž dz & + \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz & \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Calculations

Begin with the Hodge dual in column form

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \begin{bmatrix} B^x \; dt Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; dt Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; dt Ôłž dz \\ \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz \\ \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Duplicate each 2-form

The following line is so obvious that the prospective reader may be confused. I am; indeed; really writing that \(A = \frac{1}{2} (A+A)\).

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \begin{bmatrix} B^x \; dt Ôłž dx & + B^x \; dt Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; dt Ôłž dy & + B^y \; dt Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; dt Ôłž dz & + B^z \; dt Ôłž dz \\ \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz & + \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz \\ \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx & + \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy & + \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Flip the exterior product

The purpose of the above operation was to utilize the antisymmetry of the exterior product and flip the signs \(dx^╬╝ Ôłž dx^╬Ż = -dx^╬Ż Ôłž dx^╬╝\) as needed.

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \begin{bmatrix} B^x \; dt Ôłž dx & - B^x \; dx Ôłž dt \\ B^y \; dt Ôłž dy & - B^y \; dy Ôłž dt \\ B^z \; dt Ôłž dz & - B^z \; dz Ôłž dt \\ \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz & - \E^x \; dz Ôłž dy \\ \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx & - \E^y \; dx Ôłž dz \\ \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy & - \E^z \; dy Ôłž dx \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

The purpose of this operation is to switch the representation of the dual Faraday 2-Form as a single row of basis 2-Forms, to a row/column representation.

Reorder into rows/column representation

From there, we conclude utilizing the free matrix representation of the Cartan-Hodge formalism, reordering the elements into rows and columns.

\[\begin{split}G^{ÔÖşÔÖş} = \begin{bmatrix} & - B^x \; dx Ôłž dt & - B^y \; dy Ôłž dt & - B^z \; dz Ôłž dt \\ B^x \; dt Ôłž dx & & - \E^z \; dy Ôłž dx & + \E^y \; dz Ôłž dx \\ B^y \; dt Ôłž dy & + \E^z \; dx Ôłž dy & & - \E^x \; dz Ôłž dy \\ B^z \; dt Ôłž dz & - \E^y \; dx Ôłž dz & + \E^x \; dy Ôłž dz & \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
